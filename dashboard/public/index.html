PreSend Adaptive Decision Guard Framework (PADGF)

Demonstration Dashboard - Evaluation Matrix

Methodological Note: PADGF operates strictly as a client-side pre-broadcast risk evaluation and decision framework. It generates a pre-broadcast sandwich attack exposure estimate based on simulation outputs. It does not observe real live mempool behavior, does not guarantee transaction safety, and does not eliminate MEV. The normalized risk score evaluates exposure parameters but does not prove an attack will definitively occur.

2. Custom Test Scenario (Exploratory Demonstration)

Note: The fixed thesis scenario is used for reproducible evaluation. Custom scenarios are exploratory demonstrations and are not used as the official Chapter 4 baseline. Results are saved separately under results/custom_runs/.

Methodological Consistency: The PADGF framework acts as the decision-making authority. The evaluation does not manually select or predetermine outcomes. Instead, the framework performs pre-broadcast simulation on the chosen parameters, calculates relevant risk indicators, computes the normalized risk score, and automatically derives the final decision (Execute, Delay, or Block) based on the configured threshold policy. Decisions are a direct consequence of this dynamic evaluation process.
